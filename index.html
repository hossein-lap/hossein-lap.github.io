For a very long time I’ve wanted to create a weblog to write and share some stuffs in it. There we go :D

I am a 22 years-old C/C++/Lua programmer, who happens to learn groff (GNU Roff) and LaTeX, to take notes about Python programming and the fundamental of the git version control system (VCS). SysAdmin and Linux/Unix user/lover. Yet another minimalist person with a passion about Free (As in Freedom) Software.


I was a Computer guy from my early days back in my 1st and 2nd grade. The first PC which my dad bought (I was ~3 years-old at the time) had an Intel Pentium III Processor, with 128MB DDR1 RAM and 32~64MB GPU. (Q: How do I know these? A: I found the motherboard years later on the backyard). But all I did was playing video games. Until my dad came to me and said:

Don’t just play video games kiddo, it can do much more than that. Do not wory about messing up the system, everything will be fine after a simple windows reinstall. Try to learn it.

And that was it. It became the goal of my life :)
